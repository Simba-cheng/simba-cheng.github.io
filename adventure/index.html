1.3. The Little Red Hen

  • 内容
  • 中文
  • 重点词汇
  • 来源

Once upon a time, there was a little red hen. She lived on a farm with a dog, a cat, and a duck. One day, she found some wheat. She said, “Who will help me plant this wheat?” The dog said, “Not I.” The cat said, “Not I.” The duck said, “Not I.” So, the little red hen planted the wheat herself. Later, the wheat grew tall. She worked hard and made bread from it. Then she ate the bread all by herself.

从前，有一只红色的小母鸡。她住在一个农场里，和一只狗、一只猫、一只鸭子一起生活。有一天，她发现了一些小麦。她说：“谁来帮我种这些小麦？”狗说：“我不帮。”猫说：“我不帮。”鸭子说：“我不帮。”于是，红色的小母鸡自己种了小麦。后来，小麦长得很高。她努力工作，用小麦做了面包。然后，她一个人把面包吃掉了。

2. Tom不再挑食的故事

  • 内容
  • 中文
  • 重点词汇
  • 来源

Tom was a very picky man. He didn’t want to eat just anything, especially bananas, even though they were his mother’s favorite fruit.

One day, his mother said, ‘Tom, if you want to grow big and strong, you need to pay attention to what you eat.’

Tom thought about it while riding his bike to school. He looked at his lunchbox and saw a banana, a pencil, and a small lamp his mom had packed by mistake.

At lunchtime, his friend Max said, ‘Tom, why don’t you eat the banana? It’s good for you, and it will give you energy to continue playing after school.’

Tom finally decided to try it. He opened his mouth and took a bite. To his surprise, the banana tasted really good!

From that day on, Tom wasn’t so picky anymore. He even started to enjoy other fruits and vegetables, especially when his mom reminded him to eat well.

汤姆是个非常挑剔的人。他不想吃任何东西，尤其是香蕉，尽管那是他妈妈最喜欢的水果。

有一天，他妈妈说：“汤姆，如果你想长得又高又强壮，你需要注意你吃的东西。”

汤姆骑着自行车去学校时想了想。他看着他的午餐盒，里面有一根香蕉、一支铅笔，还有一个他妈妈误装进去的小台灯。

午餐时间，他的朋友马克斯说：“汤姆，你为什么不吃香蕉呢？它对你有好处，还能让你放学后有精力继续玩。”

汤姆终于决定试一试。他张开嘴咬了一口。令他惊讶的是，香蕉的味道真的很好！

从那天起，汤姆不再那么挑剔了。他甚至开始喜欢其他水果和蔬菜，尤其是当他妈妈提醒他要吃得健康时。
